The method

Three steps. No fluff.

Most platforms hand you a syllabus and disappear. Chalkline is built around finishing things.

  1. 01

    Pick a skill, not a subject

    Courses are grouped by what you'll be able to do afterward — not by vague academic headings.

  2. 02

    Learn in working sessions

    Every lesson is built around one task you can realistically finish in under twenty minutes.

  3. 03

    Build something you can show

    Each course ends in a real project for your portfolio — a certificate is just the receipt.
